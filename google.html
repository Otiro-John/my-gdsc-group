Who We Are?

Welcome to the Google Students Developers Club at Kabete, where innovation meets opportunity. Our club is dedicated to providing students with the tools and network needed to excel in the tech industry. We offer a range of activities including workshops, hackathons, and mentorship programs designed to foster both personal and professional growth.
